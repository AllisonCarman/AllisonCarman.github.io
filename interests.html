WHAT I DO WITH MY LIFE

  • Dunkin' Donuts: This is one of my favorite pastimes. I like to consider myself somewhat of a coffee connoisseur. After all, coffee is happiness.
  • Ugly Betty and Desperate Housewives: These are two of my favorite shows. I have invested countless hours in keeping up with the fictional characters of these shows.
  • Going to museums: I frequent the Levine Museum and the Mint Museum uptown. Their exhibits always intrigue me, and sometimes I get extra credit for class by attending!
  • Exploring: I enjoy taking long walks around the uptown area, as well as taking drives when I am bored and tired of being confined to my house.
